Desmond is excited to embrace this new chapter in life, learning to become a fullstack developer. Having

learned HTML at the age of 13 through chatrooms (and eventually myspace), Desmond went on to take

an HTML and two C++ courses in high-school.

Desmond would not pursue coding and software development in college however, as he pursued his passion in theater and story-telling, and went on to be an actor and live show performer. Over the years he's performed domestically and abroad on stage and film.


Often thinking back to his teenage interest in coding (and linguistics in his 20s) the perfect storm of being an out of work entertainer, in quarantine, enjoying producing digital content, as well as a general nostalgic sentiment towards coding in his teens, Desmond lept at the opportunity to formerly train as a developer.



Desmond is of course thrilled to see where this new career path will lead, and how it could potentially enhance and incorporate his creative and artistic leanings. Thanks for reading! More content coming soon!
